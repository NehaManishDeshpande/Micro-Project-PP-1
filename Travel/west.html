West India.

The western part of India is blessed with scenic views, long coastlines, magnificent religious centres and a truck-load of hospitality, making it one of the most popular travel destinations in India. It comprises of Goa, Gujarat, Maharashtra, Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu.
